About Me

Building Intelligent Solutions

I'm Melaku Tilahun, an award-winning full-stack developer and AI/ML engineer focused on building practical, secure, and scalable digital solutions. My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, artificial intelligence, and applied systems, with hands-on experience delivering products in education, health, digital identity, and security.

I am a Tech Innovator at the Jimma University Innovation Incubation Center and a member of YeneHealth Tech PLC, where I serve as Campus Volunteer Coordinator. I build across web, mobile, AI, and security domains, with a strong bias toward clean architecture, developer experience, and real-world deployment.

What drives me? Taking complex challenges and turning them into simple, effective tech solutions that people actually use. My work has been recognized in several innovation competitions, but the real reward is seeing the impact of what I build on real users.

I'm always open to interesting collaborations, innovative ideas, or meaningful tech conversations. If you're working on something that matters, let's connect.
